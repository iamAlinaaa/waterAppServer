Default water goal: 2L

This is a very simple water drinking desktop app to monitor how much water you consume. All you need is to enter your current weight, age, gender and sport activity hours per day, and Water app will help you determine how much water your body needs daily.

Each time you take a glass of water, you just need to choose an amount of water you drink and press "+" button to update your progress. If you made a mistake or accidentally added more water than you've drank, just choose amount of liquid you want to remove and press "-" button.

You can also drink 2 liters of water by default.

Drinking 2 litres of water a day cleanses toxins from your body, gives you energy and helps with fatigue. Staying hydrated can bring a good dose of brain power and vitality to your day. It also helps your kidneys regain balance.

But we recommend you to calculate your own water balance that depends on your activity and weight.
